********************************************************************************************************

/trained_ANN_models/ - ANN models used to speed up process optimisatons. Separate ANN models are trained
                       for each of the proces-level KPIs, i.e., CO2 purity and recovery, energy, and 
                       productivity

********************************************************************************************************

plot_performance_for_publication.m - script to visualise the prediction performance of ANN model (rep-
                                     roduces figure 3 of the main article)

plotTrainingData.m - script to visualise the distribution of data used for training ANN.

trainEnergyProd.m - script to optimise the performance of ANN models which predict energy and produc-
                    tivity process-level KPIs. 

trainPuRe.m - script to optimise the performance of ANN models which predict CO2 purity and recovery 
              process-level KPIs.

********************************************************************************************************
